Product Name: QuickPrep Smart Meal Kit

Category: Food / Convenience / Subscription

Description:
QuickPrep is a weekly meal kit subscription designed for people with limited time who still want to eat better without cooking from scratch.

Each box includes pre-portioned ingredients and partially pre-cooked components that allow you to prepare meals in under 15 minutes. No advanced cooking skills are required.

The meals are healthier than typical fast food, but not strictly diet-focused. The emphasis is on convenience, time-saving, and reducing daily decision fatigue.

Price:
$12 per meal (average weekly cost: $60–$84 depending on plan)

Target Use Case:
Busy professionals, students, or small households who often rely on takeout but want a slightly healthier and more structured alternative.

Key Value Proposition:
- Saves time during weekdays
- Reduces reliance on delivery apps
- Requires minimal effort

Differentiation:
Unlike traditional meal kits, QuickPrep minimizes cooking time by including partially prepared ingredients. However, it is more expensive than cooking from scratch and less customizable than premium meal services.

Limitations:
- Limited menu variety per week
- Requires refrigeration space
- Not ideal for large families
